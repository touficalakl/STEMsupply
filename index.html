About STEMSupply

STEMSupply is a supply chain management system designed specifically for STEM fields, offering both products (e.g., lab equipment, electronic components, software licenses) and services (e.g., device calibration, lab maintenance, prototyping services). The goal of the system is to streamline the procurement process, ensuring timely delivery, transparency in supply chains, and optimized inventory management.
